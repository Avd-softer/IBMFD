Small Steps, Big Impact
by Chetna Sarvankar

Date: July 29, 2025

Taking care of mental health doesn’t require big changes—sometimes, tiny steps make the biggest difference. A deep breath, a message to a friend, or simply getting out of bed is meaningful.

Resilience grows when we choose self-care and reach out for support. Tough days happen but don’t define us.

Remember: you’re not alone. Celebrate each little victory—every step counts, and better days are ahead!
